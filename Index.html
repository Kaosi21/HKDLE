Hollowdle Main Page

Character challenge

This game challenges you to guess a Hollow Knight character based on this attributes:

  • Name: The character's name.
  • Region: The area or kingdom where the character is found.
  • Alignment: Good = You can only talk with him , Neutral = You can both talk and fight, Hostile = You can only fight him.
  • Species: The species or race of the character.
  • Weapon: The weapon/abilities the character uses.
  • Gameplay: NPC , Boss , Normal enemy.
  • Gender: The character's gender.

Charms challenge

This game challenges you to guess a Hollow Knight charm based on this attributes:

  • Name: The charm's name.
  • Category: Offensive = do some damage, Defensive = defend/heal you, Supportive = makes more efficient something.
  • Location: The area or kingdom where the charm is found.
  • Cost: The cost of the charm.
  • Obtained: If the charm is buyed, gifted or obtained.

Zoomed challenge

You have to recognize the character based on his zoomed image, every error makes the image less zoomed
